STORY

In a war-torn world, you are a highly skilled cryptographer caught between duty and morality. Forced into service and tasked with decrypting enemy communications, you uncover hidden truths and witness the atrocities committed by both sides. As you receive anonymous messages from a mysterious source, the lines of loyalty blur, and you question your role in the war. Will you stay loyal or risk everything to uncover the manipulations and fight for justice? Experience the power of intelligence, and the weight of your choices in this immersive wartime cryptography thriller.

GAMEPLAY

Decrypt codes, Deduce intel, Doctor messages.
In Insignia you will gain intelligence, ultilise it and perform counter intelligence to gain the upperhand in the war effort.
But you cannot act on all things.
Burn messages, or send them. The choice is yours.
How it will affect the fate of your Brother is in your hands.
